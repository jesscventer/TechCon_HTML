Our History

TechCon began in 2001 as a small gathering of 42 tech enthusiasts in a local library. Our initial goal was to improve knowledge sharing and collaboration among emerging professionals. Sinc our humble beginnings we have grown into the premier event in Africa, and if we may say so - the world! Our conferences now attract attendees and speakers from over 100 countries.

TechCon always aims to adapt to the rapidly changing technological landscape, always striving to bring the most cutting-edge topics and influential voices to our audience.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to empower the global tech community by providing an unparalleled platform for learning, innovation, and networking. We are dedicated to:

  • Bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and practical application.
  • Fostering a diverse and inclusive environment for all tech professionals.
  • Inspiring the next generation of technologists and innovators.
  • Driving discussions that shape the future of technology responsibly.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Any One

Dr. Any One is a renowned AI ethicist and lead researcher at Global Minds Inc. At TechCon 2022, she delivered a powerful keynote on "AI and the Future of Human Society," challenging us to consider the ethical implications of emerging AI technologies.

Mr. Been Too Keen

A cybersecurity veteran and founder of SecureNet Solutions, Mr. Too Keen spoke at TechCon 2021 about "Defending Against Next-Gen Cyber Threats." His insights into proactive security measures were invaluable.

Ms. Sunny Days

Ms. Days, a cloud architect at CloudScape Innovations, shared her expertise on "Optimizing Cloud Infrastructure for Scalability" at TechCon 2023. Her practical advice on serverless architectures was a highlight.
